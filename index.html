ABOUT US

Telemetry Nigeria Limited is an indigenous company specializing in Custody Transfer Accuracy Measurement, Oil and Gas Measuring Equipment installation, Calibration and Verification in the Oil /Gas Industry in Nigeria. The company was incorporated in 2001 to offer consultancy, engineering, legal metrology services and allied services in the oil /gas industry and other sectors of the economy. The company is a leading organization in the field of fair and accurate measurement in custody transfer transactions in the various sectors of the economy most especially the oil sector. The company has its headquarters at No 6 Offa Close Oyo Street Garki Abuja.
